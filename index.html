HOW TO PLAY

You're an elite naval aviator. Out-fly your rival and score 3 round wins to take the match.

  • GUNS — short range, must be aimed. Fire in bursts — hold the trigger too long and they OVERHEAT and lock out for a while. Ease off and they cool on their own.
  • FOX TWO (IR missile) — hold the enemy in your nose cone until LOCK, then fire. Chases heat; 4 per round with a short reload between launches.
  • FLARES — pop hot decoys to spoof an inbound missile. Flare AND break hard together to shake it.

Energy: slow = tight turns, fast = wide. Afterburner is fast but lights you up like a beacon on IR — easy to lock. Watch for MISSILE warnings and defend!

Ships (carrier & destroyers) block guns, missiles and line-of-sight — use them for cover, but don't scrape the hull. Pick your arena on the menu. Stay inside the combat zone — the edges are hard walls, so no running off-screen.
